🚨 Common Issues & Solutions

  • Camera not enabled: Click the camera toggle in the popup
  • No permissions: Click camera icon in Chrome address bar → "Always allow"
  • No face detected: Ensure good lighting and face is visible
  • Score stuck at --: Check console for CV worker errors
  • Multiple setState calls: Fixed in latest version
